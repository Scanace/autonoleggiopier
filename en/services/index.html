SERVICES

Airport transfer

From wherever you come and for any destination you want to leave we will be happy to accompany you and / or pick you up at the airport. The service covers all Italian and foreign airports and can make you reach both snow-covered ski resorts and sunny seaside resorts. Transportation can be arranged for you, for your loved one or for an important guest.

Business trip or VIP day

For business trips, to enjoy an evening without the thought of driving, for long trips even more days, to be accompanied in the best shopping places without thinking of parking, rely on our services that guarantee punctuality, reliability, comfort and security. It is possible to book the service even for long periods with continuous collaboration relationships.

Excursions

Are you a sportsman? Do you need a safe support to take you to the locations where your favorite activities take place? We will take you to the place of departure, we will come to pick you up on arrival and we will keep your equipment in our means so you can enjoy your adventure with absolute carefreeness.

Turistic trips

You are on holiday? Do you want to enliven your guests with a tour of the most beautiful places in the surrounding area? We will take care to take you to admire the natural and artistic beauty of the area.

Urban-suburban taxi service

Do you want to move around the city avoiding the stress of traffic? Do you need to reach a location outside the city?
We are at your disposal to lead you in maximum comfort between the streets of the city or any other destination.

Events

Have you decided to go to a concert with friends? Are you organizing a stag / hen party? Are you thinking about a romantic evening and you want everything to be perfect?
We will make sure that your move is done in total safety and with maximum comfort and elegance.

Day in the alm

Do you want to spend a pleasant day breathing pure mountain air?
We are at your disposal to accompany you at the characteristic Trentine mountain alm of Passo Vezzena. Here you can taste succulent homemade delicacies, enjoy relaxing walks immersed in green meadows and participate in rural life with your children, letting you get involved in the experience of an educational farm.

For booking your trip or request further information please contact us via telephone or email otherwise visit our contact page.
